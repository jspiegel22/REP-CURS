1. Getting to Cabo

Los Cabos International Airport (SJD) is the main gateway to Cabo San Lucas, with direct flights from many major cities in the United States, Canada, and Mexico. Once you arrive, there are several transportation options to get to your accommodation:

  • Airport transfers (pre-booked or on arrival)
  • Shuttle services
  • Rental cars
  • Taxis
Pro Tip: Book your airport transfer in advance to avoid the hassle at the airport and ensure a smooth start to your vacation.

2. Where to Stay

Cabo offers a wide range of accommodations to suit every preference and budget:

Luxury Resorts

Experience world-class service and amenities at Cabo's prestigious resorts, many featuring private beaches, spa facilities, and multiple dining options.

Private Villas

Perfect for families or groups, private villas offer exclusive amenities, privacy, and often come with dedicated staff.

Boutique Hotels

For a more intimate experience, boutique hotels provide unique charm and personalized service.

Top Pick: For the ultimate luxury experience, consider staying at one of the beachfront villas with stunning views of the Sea of Cortez.
